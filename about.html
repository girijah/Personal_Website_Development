The CoreSparker Story!

The CoreSparker started up in 2015, and it has been on an epic ride since the beginning. coreSparker offers an interface where achievers can post their achievements and share their experience and motivate students to achieve more. Mean while, the web site tried to stay true to its core beliefs and to deliver an exceptional experience for its community and subscribers. The site owes a huge thanks to the community for joining with it on this awesome journey, and coreSparker hopes that you'll continue to be a part of its story.
